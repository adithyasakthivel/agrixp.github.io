Flecktarn refers to a family of disruptive camouflage patterns developed in Germany in 1976. The original German 5-color flecktarn has been adopted, copied and modified by many countries for their own camouflage patterns.

Close-up of proposed camouflage design

The proposed camouflage (3 color model) design consists of two dominant shades of dark grey along with a lighter tone (which are listed below) to create visible contrast and a noticeable image of authority. 

The proposed pattern consists of the following colors: 
- Dark Grey (#020202)
- Dark Gray (#100e0f)
- Light Grey (#393939)

The ideal users of said pattern include law enforcement tactical units and other paramilitary forces operating in urban environments.
